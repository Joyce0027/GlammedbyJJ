About GlammedbyJJ

Founded in January 2025 and based in Bowie/Towson, GlammedbyJJ is committed to enhancing each clients natural beauty. Specializing in flawless makeup looks for every occasion, GlammedbyJJ combines skill, creativity, and personalized care to ensure each client booking and reciving my service feels confident and radiant. From soft, full and bold glams, every service is tailored to highlight your unique style.

Policies

Booking

  • $15 Non-Refundable Deposits
  • Appointments must be booked at least 48 hours in advance.
  • Bookings made through Booking Site.

Late Policy

  • Clients have a 10-minute grace period. After the grace period, a $5 late fee will be charged for every 5 minutes delayed.
  • After 20 minutes, your appointment will be canceled. Repeated lateness may prevent future bookings.

Payments

  • All transactions are accepted via Zelle or Cash Only. Ensure payment is completed at the end of service.
  • Your deposit secures your appointment and is applied to your total service cost.

Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • Please provide at least 24 hours’ notice if you need to cancel or reschedule.
  • No-shows can result in loss of future booking privileges.
  • Squeeze-Ins are an extra $25 added to the original price.

Group Bookings (3 or More)

  • A non-refundable deposit of $30 is required to reserve your group appointment (for groups of [3 or more people]).
  • If your group size decreases, the deposit still applies to the total cost.
  • Group Booking prices may vary.
