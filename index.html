About GlammedbyJJ

Founded in January 2025 and based in Bowie/Towson, GlammedbyJJ is committed to enhancing each clients natural beauty. Specializing in flawless makeup looks for every occasion, GlammedbyJJ combines skill, creativity, and personalized care to ensure each client booking and reciving my service feels confident and radiant. From soft, full and bold glams, every service is tailored to highlight your unique style.

The CEO

My name is Joyce Orimogunje, the artist behind GlammedbyJJ. I've always been passionate about makeup and have been growing my skills and artistry for the past five years. Combining my makeup artistry and tech skills, I've built this website as a creative extension of my brand. My vision for GlammedbyJJ is to expand, travel, and bring effortless beauty and confidence to people everywhere.
