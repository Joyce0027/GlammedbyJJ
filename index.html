About GlammedbyJJ

Founded in January 2025 and based in Bowie/Towson, GlammedbyJJ is committed to enhancing each client's natural beauty. Specializing in flawless makeup looks for every occasion, combining skill, creativity, and personalized care.

The CEO

My name is Joyce Orimogunje, the artist behind GlammedbyJJ. I've been growing my skills and artistry for the past five years. My vision is to expand, travel, and bring effortless beauty and confidence to people everywhere.
